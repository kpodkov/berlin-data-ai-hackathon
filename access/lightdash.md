# Lightdash

[Lightdash](https://www.lightdash.com/) is an open-source BI tool connected to your team's Snowflake database. Use it to build dashboards and visualisations for your analysis and final presentation.

## Connection

- **URL**: `TODO — will be provided on-site`
- **Login**: same email as your Snowflake credentials
- **Project**: your team project (Team 1, Team 2, etc.)

Each team has its own Lightdash project pre-connected to your Snowflake database via a read-only service account. The shared data tables (T1–T4, OBJECTS, PACKAGES) are accessible via `DB_JW_SHARED.CHALLENGE.*`.

## Getting started

1. Log in to Lightdash with your email
2. Select your team's project
3. Use the SQL Runner to write queries and visualise results
4. Build dashboards combining multiple charts
5. Share dashboards with your team and the judges

**Note**: Lightdash also supports dbt model exploration, but for this hackathon the SQL Runner is the fastest way to get started — no dbt project setup required.
